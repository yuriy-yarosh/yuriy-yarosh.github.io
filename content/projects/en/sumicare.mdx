[Sumicare](https://sumicare.io/) is a [Yuriy Yarosh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yuriy-yarosh-171ba3b9/) Consumer Intelligence App that detects and analyzes consumer commodity product packaging and identifies anomalies in real-time.

It introduces a new dimension to consumer product analysis through advanced AI and computer vision techniques. Including, but not limited to:

- Real-time detection and product classification using Gaussian splats and advanced semantic compression algorithms
- On-board distributed vector databases for fast and reliable feature storage
- Complete offline-first operation, for indoor use, no paywalls
